Coach Notes

Small habits that keep progress sustainable.

Before Training

Drink water, do a short mobility flow, and start with your hardest lift while energy is highest.

After Training

Refuel with protein, stretch for five minutes, and log how the session felt to adjust next time.

On Rest Days

Keep moving with walking or light mobility so your body recovers without feeling stiff or flat.
